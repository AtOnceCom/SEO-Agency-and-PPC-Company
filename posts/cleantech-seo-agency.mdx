## Cleantech SEO keywords that can be worth targeting

Cleantech buyers search in different ways. Some search for broad category terms. Others search for product specs, incentives, or vendor comparisons.

A cleantech SEO agency can build a keyword map that includes:

- **Category terms** (example: clean energy software, carbon accounting platform)  
- **Problem terms** (example: reduce emissions reporting time, energy audit automation)  
- **Comparison terms** (example: carbon management tools comparison)  
- **Integration terms** (example: integrates with ERP, integrates with utility data)  
- **Location terms** when sales involve regions (example: carbon reporting in specific markets)  

Keyword choices can depend on sales goals, deal size, and how much education a buyer requires.

## What can make cleantech SEO harder, and how planning can help

Cleantech SEO can face unique limits:
