## Why WordPress websites can struggle with SEO

WordPress can be flexible, but flexibility can create messy sites when many pages get added over time. SEO issues can come from site structure, content gaps, or content that does not match what searchers want.

Here are areas that can limit WordPress SEO performance:

- **Thin content:** Pages that do not answer the query fully  
- **Duplicate topics:** Many posts that target the same keyword without a clear main page  
- **Weak internal linking:** Important pages not linked from other posts  
- **Unclear categories:** Tags and categories that do not support a clean structure  
- **Outdated posts:** Old pages that no longer match current searches  
- **Low conversion pages:** Traffic comes in, but forms, pages, or messages do not convert well  

A WordPress SEO agency can help prioritize which issues matter first, then publish improvements in a steady way.
