## A simple way to think about Shopify search intent

Search intent matters for Google Ads. A person searching for a product name can act differently than a person searching for a broad category.

A practical campaign plan can include:

1. **Brand terms:** Searches for the brand name and branded products  
2. **Product terms:** Searches for exact product types and high-intent keywords  
3. **Category terms:** Broader collection-level searches that can be tested carefully  
4. **Problem terms:** Searches tied to a problem the product solves, if relevant  

Each layer can use different ad copy and different landing pages, which can change conversion rate.

## How wasted spend can show up in Shopify Google Ads

Spend can go to clicks that never had a real chance to buy. This can happen from mismatched keywords, weak landing pages, or unclear offers.

Areas that can be reviewed to reduce wasted spend:

- **Search terms report:** Find irrelevant searches and add negatives  
- **Match types:** Adjust broad, phrase, and exact to fit goals  
- **Geo settings:** Limit to locations that make sense for shipping and margins  
- **Device performance:** Tune bids and landing pages for mobile shoppers  
- **Budget allocation:** Shift budget away from low-quality traffic sources  

Reducing wasted spend can raise return on ad spend without increasing total budget.
