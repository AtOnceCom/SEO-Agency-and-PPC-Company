## Automotive keywords that can bring better leads

Automotive search terms can fall into different groups. When campaigns mix these groups, lead quality can drop.

A cleaner setup can involve separate campaigns for each intent. That can make results easier to measure and improve.

Keyword groups can include:

- **Buy intent** like “buy,” “price,” “lease,” and “finance”  
- **Model intent** like make, model, trim, and year searches  
- **Local intent** like “near me” and city or neighborhood names  
- **Service intent** like “oil change,” “brakes,” and “tire rotation”  
- **Parts intent** like “OEM parts” and part numbers  
- **Brand intent** like dealership names and branded searches  

AtOnce can build a keyword plan that matches real pages on a site, so the click and the page can fit together.

## Ad copy that can match the search and improve clicks

Automotive ads can compete with many brands on the same page. Ad copy can help, but only when it matches the search and stays clear.

Ad copy testing can include:

- **Offer language** like promos, pricing notes, or limited terms  
- **Trust points** like certified techs, warranties, or reviews

AtOnce can write and test ad copy, then keep the best messages and remove weak ones. That can raise click-through rate while staying accurate.
