## A simple process for done-for-you article writing

A good process can reduce delays and rework. The exact steps can vary, but a structured approach can help content stay consistent.

Here is one process that can be used:

1. **Audit and research**  
   Content gaps can be found by reviewing the site and analyzing competitors for topics, structure, and keyword opportunities.

2. **SEO content strategy**  
   A keyword plan can be created around high value topics. This can help build clusters instead of isolated posts.

3. **Writing and editing**  
   Articles can be written to match search intent, brand voice, and readability. Editing can improve clarity and flow.

4. **Publishing and optimization**  
   Content can be uploaded, formatted, internally linked, and published. Existing content can also be improved over time.
