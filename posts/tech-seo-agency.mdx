## What makes tech SEO harder than general SEO

Tech SEO can be more detailed than general SEO because the product and buyer journey can be more specific.

Challenges can include:

- **Many audiences at once** (end users, admins, leaders)
- **Many terms for the same thing** (technical terms, brand terms, generic terms)
- **Fast product changes** that can make pages outdated
- **Hard-to-explain value** when the product has many features
- **Internal approval loops** that slow content shipping

A practical approach can help reduce delays and keep content moving.
