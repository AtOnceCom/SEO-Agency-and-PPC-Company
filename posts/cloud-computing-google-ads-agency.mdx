Cloud services is a broad term. Ads can perform better when campaigns match what people search for, and when landing pages match that search.

- **Brand intent:** searches for the company name and product name

## A clear way to structure Google Ads for cloud computing

A cloud computing Google Ads account can get hard to read when too many products and keywords get mixed together. A clean structure can make budgets and results easier to understand.

A structure can involve:

1. **Separate campaigns by product line**  
   That can keep budgets stable and keep reporting clear.

2. **Separate campaigns by intent level**  
   “Demo” and “pricing” searches can act different than research searches.

3. **Separate search and display**  
   Display can help awareness, while search can capture demand.

4. **Keep ad groups tight**  
   Tight ad groups can keep ads and landing pages aligned with searches.

AtOnce can help plan a structure like this and manage it week to week.
