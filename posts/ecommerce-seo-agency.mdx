## Ecommerce SEO keywords that can bring shoppers, not just visitors

Traffic alone can be a vanity metric. Ecommerce SEO can perform better when keywords match real buying behavior.

Keyword targets can include:

- **Problem-led terms:** searches tied to a problem the product solves  
- **Comparison terms:** searches that involve alternatives and choices  
- **Use-case terms:** searches tied to where and how products get used  
- **Brand and category terms:** searches that support category authority  

This type of keyword mix can support both discovery and purchase intent, while also building a stronger site structure for search engines.
