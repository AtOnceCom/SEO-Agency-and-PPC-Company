## Keywords And Intent: The Part That Can Change Everything

Medtech keywords can be expensive, and broad terms can bring the wrong clicks. Keyword strategy can improve when it matches intent.

A practical way to group intent can involve:

- **Problem keywords:** Searches about symptoms, workflows, or clinical problems  
- **Solution keywords:** Searches about categories like imaging, monitoring, or diagnostics  
- **Product keywords:** Searches for a device name, model, or product type  
- **Buying keywords:** Searches that include pricing, vendors, quotes, or suppliers  
- **Comparison keywords:** Searches that compare options or list alternatives  

AtOnce can build campaigns around these groups so ads and landing pages stay aligned. When alignment improves, conversion rates can improve too.
