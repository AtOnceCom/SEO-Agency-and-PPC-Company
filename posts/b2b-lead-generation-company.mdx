## What “B2B lead generation” can mean in real terms

B2B lead generation can involve creating steady demand from people searching for solutions, then guiding them to take a clear action, like filling out a form or requesting a demo.

Leads can come from more than one channel, including:

- **Search (SEO):** Content can rank for keywords that match buyer intent.

When these parts connect well, lead generation can be more predictable and easier to measure.

## Lead generation can improve when keywords match buyer intent

A balanced keyword plan can support both demand capture and demand creation, depending on the market and how crowded search results are.
