## A content plan that can match how logistics buyers search

Many buyers search in steps. Early searches can be about learning. Later searches can be about vendors, pricing, and service details.

An SEO plan can cover both stages with:

1. **Service and solution pages**  
   Pages can explain services, coverage areas, industries served, and what the process involves.

2. **Buyer question content**  
   Articles can answer questions about transit times, documents, shipment types, and how services differ.

3. **Comparison and decision content**  
   Content can cover choices like LTL vs FTL, broker vs carrier, or in house vs outsourced fulfillment.

4. **Proof and trust content**  
   Content can support case studies, certifications, safety and compliance notes, and service standards.

AtOnce can plan a full content map so each article supports a bigger topic and links into the right money pages.
